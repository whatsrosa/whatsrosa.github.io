Welcome!

My name is Rosa Fsadni, a graphic designer that loves to explore and experiment. As a designer, I have always looked for the best way to find the exact means of expression.



I've always had a passion for all things visual. I enjoy exploring the world of design in all of its forms, including illustration, graphic design, and digital art. I'm presently concentrating on learning more about photography, film and animation.

I'm a Maltese student now pursuing a graphic design degree, and I aim to always provide the best work for customers in order to always develop my design expertise.

I always aspire to be the greatest version of myself, learning and communicating with new individuals in order to obtain new experiences and establish stronger relationships with others.
